# FundBridge
Fundbridge is a Marketplace platform that helps connect Government Backed Startups with Private Investors.
We bridge the gap between government-backed early-stage startups and private investors in Germany.


## The Problem we solve
Alot of Promising startups that have already been validated and funded through government programs (stages 1–4) struggle to attract private capital to scale further. Private investors, on the other hand, are hesitant to invest in early-stage companies because the risk is too high and the information asymmetry is too large.
FundBridge fixes all sides of this.

## For Investors: 
1.Every startup on the platform has already passed government scrutiny, so due diligence is partially done.
2.The Investors have the Special option for a milestone based funding approach, meaning that they have control over how the investment flows to the startups, they set the milestone conditions for when money should be released and also are in control of how much money is released after each milestone reached by the startup.  
3.If the startup fails, investors get a 30% tax rebate from the amount they have invested into the startup via Fundbridge, meaningfully reducing downside risk.

## For the Government: 
1.It retains a passive equity stake (1–10%) in each startup it has supported, so public money that helped build these companies participates in the upside if they succeed.

## For the Startups: 
1.They get access to a structured marketplace where private investors can find them, evaluate them with transparent data (milestones achieved, KPI's and government support history), and submit formal investment proposals.

# In short: 
### It's a trust layer that makes government-validated startups investable for private capital, with built-in risk protection on both sides.
